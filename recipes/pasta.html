Pepper Pesto Parmesan Pasta

Ingredients

  • Penne (or pasta of your choice)
  • Pesto
  • Olive Oil
  • Crushed Red Pepper & Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil & drain pasta
  2. Re-add pasta to pot/pan and mix in olive oil and pesto
  3. Top with parmesan cheese and crushed red pepper

Enjoy!
