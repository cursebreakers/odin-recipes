Vodka-French Lasagna

    Ingredients

  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Red Sauce
  • Vodka
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Grilled Chicken
  • Artichoke Hearts
  • Spinach
  • Feta, Mozzarella & Provelone Cheese
  • Fresh Chopped Parsley & Parmesan Cheese

    Steps

  1. Par-boil pasta, grill chicken, and saute artichoke hearts.
  2. Add sauce to saute. Mix in vodka, cream and feta.
  3. Layer all ingredients, top with provelone and mozzarella.
  4. Bake for 45min at 350 degrees.
  5. Cool and serve with parsley and parmesan.

Enjoy!
